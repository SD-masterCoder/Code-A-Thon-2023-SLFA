Our Goal

Knowledge in sign language is a vital tool in order to communicate and understand people who lack the ability to hear or speak.

Our goal here at SLFA is to make American Sign Language(ASL) education more accessible to everyone. Sign Language For All (SLFA) uses machine learning and image recognition to provide an accessible platform where people can learn and pratice ASL from anywhere!

Start Learning Sign Language!

Put your hand up to the camera to practice basic sign language. Can the computer recognize what you are trying to say? If so, you are doing well!
